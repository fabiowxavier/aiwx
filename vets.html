What We Do

At AiWX, we provide expert consultancy on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and clinical data within the veterinary industry. Our key activities focus on empowering veterinary practices, technology companies, and industry stakeholders with Strategic AI Adoption, Data Optimisation, and Ethical Guidance.

AI Adoption for Veterinary Practices

We help veterinary clinics and hospitals understand, evaluate, and implement AI solutions that enhance efficiency, clinical quality, and patient care. Our support includes:

  • Education & awareness: Ethical AI use, tool evaluation, and best practices.
  • Needs assessment: Identifying AI opportunities based on clinic pain points.
  • Tailored AI recommendations: Solutions for diagnostics, workflows, and data utilisation.
  • Implementation guidance: Support through planning, selection, and ongoing optimisation.

Data Optimisation & Utilisation

We enable veterinary practices to unlock the full potential of their clinical data for smarter decision-making and business growth:

  • Data-driven insights: Structuring and analysing unstructured clinical data.
  • Optimising data strategies: Enhancing patient care and operational efficiency.
  • Performance improvement: Using data to elevate clinical quality and customer experience.
