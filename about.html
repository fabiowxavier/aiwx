On a mission to empower Ai in the Veterinary Sector

Dr Adele Williams-Xavier is a veterinary industry expert in AI and clinical data, with extensive experience in leadership within veterinary technology. As a former R&D director and SLT member, she has led the development of AI tools in pet care and has deep expertise in utilising data for meaningful insights.

An RCVS and European equine internal medicine specialist veterinarian, Adele combines her clinical expertise with a PhD and a robust track record in clinical research and education. She has worked in diverse veterinary practices, served as a senior lecturer, designed innovative veterinary curricula, and successfully managed grant applications, research reviews, and business fundraising. Passionate about improving animal care and veterinary workflows, Adele excels in leading complex projects and fostering innovation.

Values

Empower veterinary practices and technology developers to leverage AI and clinical data for innovation, improving animal health and business outcomes while promoting ethical technology use.

  • Innovation. Driving forward-thinking solutions in veterinary care.
  • Integrity. Ensuring ethical, transparent, and professional AI practices.
  • Collaboration. Building strong partnerships with clients, industry, and academia.
  • Empowerment. Equipping clients with the tools and knowledge for confident AI adoption.
  • Excellence. Delivering the highest standards in consultancy, education, and implementation.

Mission

Transform the veterinary industry by driving ethical AI adoption and clinical data optimisation to improve animal health and empower stakeholders.

Vision

Be the global leader in veterinary AI and clinical data consultancy, setting benchmarks for innovation, excellence, and ethics in animal healthcare.
